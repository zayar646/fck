Welcome to Edu Hub Book Website

your one-stop destination for all things books in Myanmar

Our Mission

The mission of Edu Hub Myanmar Book Library Website is to promote education,

literacy, and cultural awareness in Myanmar through the power of books and music.

We aim to provide access to a wide variety of educational and cultural resources to

those who need it most, regardless of their financial ability. We strive to create an

inclusive platform that caters to a wide range of audience, and foster a love for reading

and music among the community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a community where access to education and culture is a basic

right for all, and where reading and music is celebrated as an integral part of everyday

life. We aim to be the leading provider of educational and cultural resources in

Myanmar and to empower the people of Myanmar to achieve their full potential

through the power of books and music.

Our Belief

We believe that by providing access to books and music

we can help to bridge cultural gaps and promote

understandingfoster creativity and critical thinking

Myanmar and to empower the people of Myanmar to achieve their full potential

and make a positive impact in the lives of the people of Myanmar.
